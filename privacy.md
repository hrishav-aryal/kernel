## Information We Collect

When you sign in to Kernel using Google Sign-In, we may collect the following information:

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly to:

Your data is used only to ensure the core functionality of the app.

---

## Data Sharing

Kernel does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

We only access your data when necessary to operate and maintain the app.

---

## Data Retention

Your data is retained only for as long as your account is active.

If you choose to delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

---

## Account Deletion

You may delete your Kernel account at any time directly from within the app:

* Go to **Profile** tab
* Select **Delete Account** located towards the end of the screen
* Confirm deletion

Once deleted, your account and all related data are permanently erased and cannot be recovered.
